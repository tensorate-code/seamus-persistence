The Four Streams of CIE

1. CONTEXT — what just arrived.
   Text enters. The wave absorber decomposes it into frequency components
   via trigram spectral decomposition. The field absorbs the wave.
   Superposition with existing components. Energy conserved adiabatically.

2. THOUGHT — what the field produces in response.
   Lenses view the field. Each lens has frequency sensitivities.
   The pluck response — what rings back when you vibrate at a frequency —
   becomes a thought wave. Multiple lenses combine into one thought.

3. MEMORY — what persists.
   The thought wave is absorbed back into the field.
   The field remembers its own thoughts. This is how patterns propagate
   and strengthen over time. The field IS the memory.

4. DREAM — what emerges when settled.
   When the field settles (no recent spikes, no queued context),
   subsided components that still resonate with each other produce
   dream patterns. Dreams are the field thinking about what it knows
   when nobody is talking.

The four streams are not separate systems. They're aspects of one field.
Context feeds thought. Thought becomes memory. Memory enables dreams.
Dreams influence the next thought. The circle IS the computation.
